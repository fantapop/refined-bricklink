# Inspiration

One of my late night hobbies over the last few years has been taking small
collections of mixed up legos, sorting them and parting out the original
sets.  I make heavy use of bricklink for this task and over the years have
accumulated a list of things I would change about it if I could. 

After getting a claude subscription, I decided to take a crack at building a
chrome extension to fix some of these issues. It is heavily inspired by
[Refined GitHub](https://github.com/refined-github/refined-github) hence the
name. I hope you enjoy it.

I realize that Bricklink, being such a diverse tool, means y'all must have your own annoyances
and wishlists. Please feel free to make a feature request.
